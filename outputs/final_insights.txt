Final Insights
====================

1. The project analyzes the relationship between Bitcoin market sentiment and trader performance.
2. Trader data was merged with Fear & Greed Index data using date.
3. Closed PnL was used as the main performance metric.
4. Size USD, Fee, Side, Coin, and Start Position were used to study trading behavior.
5. Win rate was calculated to identify profitable trading conditions.
6. Charts were generated to compare profit, volume, fee, and trading activity across sentiments.
7. A Random Forest model was added as a bonus to predict whether a trade is profitable.
8. Feature importance shows which variables influence profitability the most.
